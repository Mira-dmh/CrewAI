**Question 1:** What is the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning?  
**Framework:** Define both terms and provide an example of each.  
**Sample Answer:** Supervised learning involves training a model on labeled data, where the outcome is known, such as classifying emails as spam or not spam. In contrast, unsupervised learning deals with unlabeled data and aims to find hidden patterns, such as clustering customers based on purchasing behavior.
